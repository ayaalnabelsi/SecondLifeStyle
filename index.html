About us

Welcome to Second Life Style, where the magic of the past unfolds in the heart of Amsterdam-East! Since 2014 at Eerste van Swindenstraat 43, and proudly in 2015 at Kinkerstraat 193, we've shared our love for vintage and sustainable fashion.

Step inside for a timeless collection from the 70s to 90s, a blend of trendy denim jackets to refined vintage party dresses. Vintage is not just a style but a lifestyle that's both timeless and eco-conscious.

Experience an unforgettable shopping journey at Second Life Style, where each garment has a story. Our team guides you to hidden treasures aligning with your personality.

Join us in our sustainability initiatives, promoting clothing reuse, reducing ecological impact, and embracing eco-friendly practices. Visitors not only enjoy unique vintage fashion but also contribute to a sustainable community, making a conscious choice for the environment.

Embrace the elegance of the past, wear the stories of a bygone era. Second Life Style - where fashion meets history.
